CloudScript

CloudScript was a team project done as our final project for Dev Bootcamp. The final projectTeam lead on a self-directed final project where we chose to build a doctor/patient communication and prescription management app that allows users to video chat or message their doctor in real time while also allowing patients to fill their prescriptions and have it delivered under 90 minutes. We chose to develop this on web and mobile with completely brand new technologies including io.js, AngluarJS, Ionic, Node/Express, MongoDB, Gulp, WebRTC and AWS.
